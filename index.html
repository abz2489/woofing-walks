About Us

Dog Walking

Covering all areas of the Rhondda Valleys, we offer friendly and reliable dog walking services that suit you best. We walk from 7am to 4.30pm Monday to Saturday. Our week day rate is £10 per hour, we charge £3 per hour extra for each additional dog. Our weekend rate is £15 per hour, with the same £3 per hour charge for additional dogs.

Doggy Daycare

We'll collect your dog between 8:30am and 9:30am, take the short journey over to our farmland and head out for some exploring. Your dog will definitely burn some energy and enjoy getting wet and muddy along the way. We drop your dog off between 2:30pm and 3:00pm. Weekday day care costs £20 for the day. Saturday day care costs £25 for the day.
