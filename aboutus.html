Our Story

In 2017, Sarah took the plunge and started a small dog walking business based in Tonyrefail. Six years on and she's now the proud owner of the ever growin Woofing Walks, offering a range of services throughout the Rhondda Valleys. Woofing Walks now has a whole team of trained dog handlers and aim to deliver the best service for each and every dog we have the pleasure of walking.

Our Doggy Day Care service has been up and running since 2020 and has been a huge success. Our dog walking service was thriving, but people were crying out for an all day service. We chose to utilise the farm land we have to build the best doggy day care facility.

Our Mission

Here at Woofing Walks we know how hard it can be juggling day to day tasks whilst catering to all of your dog's needs. Our mission is to make things easier for you the owner. Our friendly, reliable dog walking service allows you the time and space to get through the day without worrying about what mischief your pooch might be creating at home.
