Our Story

In 2017, Sarah took the plunge and started a small dog walking business based in Tonyrefail. Six years on and she's now the proud owner of the ever growing Woofing Walks, offering trusted dog walking and doggy day care services throughout the Rhondda Valleys. Woofing Walks now has a whole team of trained dog handlers and aim to deliver the best service for each and every dog we have the pleasure of walking.

Our Doggy Day Care service has been up and running since 2020 and has been a huge success. Our dog walking service was thriving, but people were crying out for an all day service. We chose to utilise the farm land we have to build the best doggy day care facility.

Our Mission

Here at Woofing Walks we know how hard it can be juggling day to day tasks whilst catering to all of your dog's needs. Our mission is to make things easier for you the owner. Our friendly, reliable dog walking service allows you the time and space to get through the day without worrying about what mischief your pooch might be creating at home.

How it Works

Contact us

Speak to a member of our team, tell us about your dogs needs. We'll explain our usual set up, and whether your requirements can be met.

If you choose to proceed we will book in for a free meet & greet session.

Meet & Greet

The meet & greet session will take up to 30 minutes. We want you to feel happy and comfortable with us. More importantly, we want to be sure that your dog is happy and comfortable with us.

Book

If all goes well at the meet and greet, we can book in your first walk.

During our walk, we'll send some photos and videos so you can see how much fun your dog is having. This also allows for peace of mind.
